Tanya's Favorite Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

A PB&J with banana is one of my favorite pick-me-ups. Hello It is delicious and requires only four main ingredients!

Photo credit: Business Insider

Ingredients

  • 2 whole wheat bread slices
  • 1 banana
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • 1 jar of strawberry jelly

Making the sandwich

  1. Toast your bread until it is of a golden brown color.
  2. Place your toasted bread slices side by side.
  3. With a butter knife, spread a generous amount of peanut butter on one slice.
  4. Repeat step three with the other slice, only this time we will be using strawberry jelly instead of peanut butter.
  5. Peel the banana and slice it horizontally as to make small circular slices.
  6. Place the banana slices on your bread slices and close your sandwich.
  7. Enjoy your tasty treat!
